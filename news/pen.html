So, What Even Is a Juicy Pen?



Written by Dr. Lemon, 3.19.23, 2:00 PM EDT


A juicy pen is a very special pen. This pen is usually an ink pen, although it can sometimes be a fancy ballpoint. Now, I know what you are thinking, "A pen? He wrote a whole artical about a pen?" Yes. Yes I did. Because these are no ordinary pens. These, are the Juicy Pens!
